Why Choose WaveRiders

At WaveRider, we're passionate about helping you ride the waves with confidence and joy.

Certified bushy-tailed instructors

Our nutty instructions are fully certified in squirrel surf safety and bring a tree-load of experience to every lesson. They are not just acorn-catching pros on the waves-they're also friendly, patient and know how to make even the shyets squirrel feel at ease.You'll feel right at home whether you're a curious kit or a seasoned tree-hopper.

Tiny class sizes for top-notch nut-struction

We keep our classes small-just a pawful of squirrels at a time-so everystudent gets plenty of one-on-one attention. This way, your instructor can focus on your unique skills and help you scurry up the learning curve faster than you can say "wave-nut!".

Flexible lesson times-Solo or with scurry

Whether you're an early riser or a sunset shredder, we've got flexible lesson times to match your tree-hopping schedule. Join a group class for some tail-slapping fun with fellow wave-riders or go for a private lesson if you prefer a quieter beach and full focus on your form.
